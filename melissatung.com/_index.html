Who I Am

My name is Melissa Tung. I’m a tea drinker. I like summer, cats, ice cream and all kinds of exercise. I like to have music on while I practice. In my world, happiness is about balance and outlook.

I help you do yoga.

Yogini since 2004, my teacher training is from Downward Dog Yoga Centre in Toronto, Canada. I’m always learning to offer you more.

Connect with me on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, & Instagram.

Services

Experienced in providing a wide range of services to fit your needs.

1-1

One-on-one instruction to delve deeper into your practice, set good foundations, complement a sport focus, or learn modifications for injuries or special need.

Private

Semi-private lessons (2-4 participants) to learn with family or friends in a setting and time of your choice with personal attention.

Corporate

Corporate classes providing team building and healthy habits with office specific stretches - added value as a work benefit.

Promotional

Promotional yoga such as yoga demonstrations or public/client instruction to add the yoga lifestyle component and visual appeal to your event.

Events

Special event yoga, perfect for group get-togethers. A fun atmosphere encouraging participation, and optional partner work where themes can be arranged.

• Package rates available for all options.
• All levels and ages welcome.
